What are Facebook Messenger Chatbots?

A Messenger Chatbot is AI software that integrates with Facebook, and can be programmed to answer your customers questions, provide more information, or help them make a purchase.

Similar to Email Marketing

The way that Messenger Chatbots works as a marketing tool is similar to email marketing. You have an account with a software provider, you ask customers to subscribe, and then you’re able to send communications to those subscribers.

1:1 Conversations at Scale

So, it helps you qualify leads, generate Sales and it's basically like a one on one conversation you are having with your customer, but at scale.
